Daily Work AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2023 | Version: 2.0

Summary: Daily Work AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered productivity platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for Daily Work AI, we collect personal information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, timezone, work preferences
  • Contact Information: If you contact support or provide feedback
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions (handled securely by our payment processors)

1.2 Productivity Data

To provide our AI-powered services, we collect data about your productivity patterns:

  • Schedule Data: Tasks, events, appointments, and daily schedules
  • Habit Information: Habit tracking data, completion rates, streaks
  • Focus Sessions: Pomodoro timer data, focus duration, break patterns
  • Notes & Documents: Content you create within the platform
  • Usage Patterns: How you interact with features and optimize your workflow

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, crashes
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to improve your experience (see Section 6)
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (for timezone detection)

3. AI & Machine Learning

Daily Work AI uses artificial intelligence to provide personalized productivity recommendations:

3.1 How AI Processes Your Data

  • Pattern Recognition: AI analyzes your schedule patterns to suggest optimizations
  • Predictive Analytics: Machine learning models predict your most productive times
  • Natural Language Processing: AI understands your task descriptions and notes
  • Recommendation Systems: Suggests optimal schedules based on your goals

3.2 Data Anonymization

For AI training and improvement:

  • Personal identifiers are removed from training data
  • Data is aggregated and anonymized before AI processing
  • We never use your personal data to train third-party AI models
  • You can opt-out of AI training data collection in settings

3.3 AI Transparency

We believe in transparent AI:

  • You can view AI reasoning for schedule suggestions
  • Explanation features show why AI made specific recommendations
  • You have full control to accept, modify, or reject AI suggestions

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and only share data in limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party services:

  • Hosting Services: Firebase (Google Cloud) for data storage
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for subscription payments
  • Communication: Email service providers for notifications

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law:

  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To investigate fraud or security issues
  • In connection with business transfers (mergers, acquisitions)

4.3 What We Never Do

  • ❌ Never sell your personal data to third parties
  • ❌ Never share your productivity data with advertisers
  • ❌ Never use your content for marketing without permission
  • ❌ Never disclose your specific schedule details publicly

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

5.1 Technical Security

  • Encryption: All data in transit uses TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Storage: Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing (bcrypt) and 2FA options
  • Firewall: Network-level protection and DDoS mitigation
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

5.2 Access Controls

  • Role-Based Access: Strict access controls for our team
  • Minimum Privilege: Employees only access data necessary for their role
  • Monitoring: Logging and monitoring of data access
  • Training: Security awareness training for all employees

5.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data archived after 2 years of inactivity
  • Account Deletion: Complete data deletion upon request (GDPR Article 17)
  • Backups: Encrypted backups retained for 30 days for disaster recovery

6.1 Essential Cookies

  • Security: Protect against cross-site request forgery
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and preferences

6.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Understand how users interact with our platform
  • Performance: Monitor page load times and errors
  • Feature Usage: Track which features are most valuable

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

  • Browser settings (block or delete cookies)
  • Our cookie consent banner (when applicable)
  • Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
  • Ad personalization settings in Google Account

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

7.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

7.2 Regional Rights

  • GDPR (EU/UK): All rights under Articles 15-22
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): Right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale
  • LGPD (Brazil): Similar rights to GDPR
  • PIPEDA (Canada): Access and correction rights

7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your privacy rights:

  1. Use the privacy controls in your account settings
  2. Submit a request through our Data Request Portal
  3. Email us at privacy@dailyworkai.com
  4. Contact our Data Protection Officer (details below)

We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Daily Work AI is not intended for children under 16:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16
  • If we learn we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's data
  • Educational versions for students have additional privacy protections

9. International Data Transfers

Daily Work AI is a global service with data processing worldwide:

  • Primary Storage: United States (Google Cloud servers)
  • Backup Locations: Multiple global regions for redundancy
  • EU Data: Protected by Standard Contractual Clauses
  • UK Data: Protected by UK Addendum to SCCs
  • Adequacy Decisions: We rely on adequacy decisions where applicable
